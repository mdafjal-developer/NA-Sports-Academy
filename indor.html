Indoor sports are a fun and effective way to stay active, socialize, and develop skills without worrying about the weather. Here are some of the most popular indoor games people love to play.

Table Tennis

A high-speed game requiring quick reflexes and precision. Played with small paddles and a lightweight ball across a netted table.

Badminton

Fast-paced game with rackets and shuttlecock. Great for building stamina, coordination, and agility indoors.

Chess

A timeless game of strategy and planning. Improve your focus, foresight, and critical thinking with every move.

Carrom

Popular in Indian households, this strike-and-pocket board game is perfect for family entertainment and competition.

Squash

An intense racket sport played against walls. It's fast, physically demanding, and great for cardio and reflexes.
